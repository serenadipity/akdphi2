Timeless Friendship Through Sisterhood

Welcome to the website of alpha Kappa Delta Phi, Lambda Chapter. alpha Kappa Delta Phi was first founded in 1990 at UC Berkeley, and since then has rapidly expanded internationally.

In Winter 1995, the Lambda Chapter of aKDPhi was created at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, with the mission to promote sisterhood, leadership, scholastic excellence, philanthropy, and Asian-American awareness within the university and the community. Feel free to learn more about our chapter and our organization by browsing the rest of this website.
